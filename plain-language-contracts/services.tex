\newcommand{\proofreading}{
\subsection*{Proofreading}
Proofreading: 3 cents per word or \$35 per hour. Proofreading is a surface-level, cosmetic treatment of the material that looks for defects in the composition that would affect the ease with which someone can read the text. Examples include misplaced commas, errors in hyphenation, line breaks, and punctuation, as well as minor stylistic flaws.
}

%———————————————————————————————————————
\newcommand{\copyediting}{
\subsection*{Copy Editing}
Copy Editing: 4-5 cents per word or \$50-60 per hour. Copy editing can be performed at a range of intensities. It is a cut above proofreading and corrects for grammatical and cosmetic mistakes but also ensures that the text is accurate, consistent, and flows well. Sentences may be rearranged, words changed, and facts checked.
